### **Method 2: Form Entry (When You Have Receipt)**

Use this when you want more control or have a receipt to scan.

#### **Steps:**

1. **Tap the camera/receipt button** in the app
2. **Take photo of receipt** (must use camera, not gallery)
3. **AI reads everything:**
   - Merchant name
   - Date, invoice number
   - All items and prices
   - Tax, service charge, totals
4. **Review the extracted data** in the form
5. **Confirm and save**

## üîß **Troubleshooting**

### **"AI didn't understand me"**
- Be more specific with amount: "grab 15" not "grab"
- Use simple words: "spend" "paid" "sold" "bought"
- Try different phrasing: "I spend 20 in mamak" ‚Üí "mamak 20"

### **"Wrong category"**
- Edit it in the review form before saving
- Or just save and edit later in transaction list

### **"Wrong amount"**
- AI might misread "15.50" as "1550"
- Always review before confirming!
- Use clear numbers: "15 point 50" or "15.5"

### **"Want to add more details"**
- Use form entry instead of chat
- Or add after saving (edit transaction)
